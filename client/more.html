What is Carbon FootPrint ??

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide, released into the atmosphere as a result of human activities, such as transportation, energy use, and food production. It measures the impact of an individual or organization on climate change, emphasizing the need for eco-friendly practices.

What are the Consequences ??

High carbon footprints contribute to climate change, causing rising temperatures, extreme weather, and disruptions to ecosystems. This leads to biodiversity loss, resource scarcity, and social conflicts. Mitigating carbon footprints is crucial for a sustainable future, reducing the severe consequences of global warming on the environment, society, and economy.
